What is Brainhack?


Brainhack is a unique conference that gathers researchers from across the globe and various disciplines to work together on innovative projects related to neuroscience. Unlike previous Brainhack events, which didn't have a theme, this event will be focused on projects related to reliability and reproducibility in connectomics.




Learn more at brainhack.org.
